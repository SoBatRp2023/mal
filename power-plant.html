A Cleaner Path Forward: Embracing Biomass as our Fuel of Choice.

As a responsible company, we are committed to reducing our carbon footprint and minimizing our impact on the environment. That's why we are making the switch from coal to biomass as our primary fuel source.

Our decision to transition from coal to biomass is a win-win for both our company and the environment. We are proud to be part of the movement towards sustainable energy and are committed to being responsible stewards of our planet.

We Support The Use of Environmentally Friendly Fuels

Why Biomass?

We support government programs to use biomass as a more environmentally friendly and sustainable energy source.

Renewable

A renewable energy source, whereas coal is a finite resource that is being depleted over time.

Reduced Emissions

Produces lower levels of sulfur dioxide, nitrogen oxides and other harmful pollutants compared to burning coal.

Improve Air Quality

Help improve air quality and reduce the negative health impacts associated with air pollution.

Local Production

Can be produced locally, which can reduce our dependence on imported fossil fuels and improve energy security.
